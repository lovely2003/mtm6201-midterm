Best places to visit in Europe

white-washed homes atop steep cliffs to deep azure waters and sun-kissed beaches

Santorini is filled with romance at every turn. The island is known for producing some of the best wines in Greece. Take a tour of a local winery and linger over a tasting. Or take a ride in a ‘kaiki’ (sailing boat) to the volcanic island of Nea Kamini for a leisurely dip in the island’s hot springs. Enjoy meals of the freshest island fare and delicious Greek specialties surrounded by breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. If you're looking for an incredibly relaxing romantic destination, Santorini is your top spot
